    Devtools

    For source modding in general, I enjoy rewriting the old vgui tools in Qt - I am currently working on a total rewrite of the SDK authoring tools, and part-way through a tool for generation of colour correction. Luckily, arsenio itself doesnt require any devtools made specifically for it, but im putting it in this catagory because I own the website, not you.

    I have also been known to rewrite the source compiler tools, specifically vrad and vbsp

    My work on vrad is mainly optimisation based, for example - in the hollowpoint branch of vrad, i have cut vrad times by ~25-30%
